Rethinking Security

Aug 8, 2020
1 min read

There are many reasons why I have started to write this article, and its an accumulation of several years of frustrations and experience when dealing with security in large organizations. First and foremost, there are two views on cybersecurity in the organization, one that the senior management has, and the one that the cybersecurity professionals do. So let’s try to light up some sparks…

The senior management view

The first thing that comes to senior management point of view is that if it isn’t the company’s core competency cybersecurity doesn’t come first. There is no argue here. If your company is in charge of distribution of goods, your key competitive advantage will not be security and that you excel at security, it’s going to be distribution of goods. PERIOD. This has been seen across multiple sectors and areas worldwide. The company is satisfied with the average or compliant status to security standards. Let’s face it, no one has time to constantly invest in cybersecurity and make it a top priority, even if it should be.
